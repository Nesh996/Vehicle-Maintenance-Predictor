Project Overview

The AI Vehicle Maintenance Predictor helps drivers detect possible vehicle problems before they become expensive failures.

The system uses simple inputs like mileage and service history to predict when parts may need maintenance.
It is designed for everyday drivers and small fleet owners, not just large logistics companies.

Ethical AI Framework

This project focuses on responsible AI, ensuring the system is fair, private, and accessible.

1. Fairness

Route Awareness
Vehicles on rough or rural roads wear out faster. The model considers road conditions so these vehicles are not unfairly judged by data from highway driving.

Confidence Score
If the system has limited data (for example with older vehicles), it shows a low confidence warning and suggests checking with a mechanic.

2. Privacy

Manual Data Input
Users enter mileage and service history themselves.
The system does not track GPS or collect personal driving data.

Fleet Privacy
For companies, the system tracks vehicle IDs instead of driver names, preventing employee surveillance.

3. Accessibility

Clear Alerts
Maintenance warnings use colors, icons, and text so everyone can understand them, including users with color vision difficulties.

Simple Language
Instead of complex diagnostic codes, the app shows clear messages like:

Brake Pad Replacement – Estimated Cost: R2,800

Environmental Impact

Predictive maintenance helps:

• reduce breakdowns
• improve fuel efficiency
• extend vehicle lifespan

This lowers the need for new vehicle production and reduces waste.

The project also recommends certified e-waste recycling for diagnostic hardware.

 Technical Features

Predictive Maintenance
Uses probability analysis to estimate when vehicle parts may fail.

Cost & Accuracy Display
Shows prediction accuracy (for example 88%) together with estimated repair costs.

Decision Support Tool
The system helps users make informed decisions but does not replace professional mechanics.

Conclusion for Users

The AI Vehicle Maintenance Predictor is designed to help you stay ahead of costly vehicle problems. By providing easy-to-understand maintenance forecasts, it empowers you to make informed decisions about repairs and service.

Remember, this app is a decision-support tool—always consult a qualified mechanic for final advice.

Your privacy is important: no GPS tracking or personal data collection is required. Just enter your mileage and service history, and the app will provide actionable insights to help your vehicle stay safe, reliable, and efficient.
